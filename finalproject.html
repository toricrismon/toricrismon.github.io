Tigers!

Tigers! They come in many shapes, sizes, and colors. They're primarily found across Asia and are the chosen mascot for many schools and universities, like Mizzou!

Below is a list of the six different types of tigers

  • Siberian tiger
  • Bengal tiger
  • South China tiger
  • Sumatran tiger
  • Indochinese tiger
  • Malayan tiger
