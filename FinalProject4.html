Appearance

When you think of a tiger, many people imagine the orange fur with black stripes and a white belly, all of these characteristics make it possible for tigers to hide in the trees or long grass while they hunt their prey.


Their fur can come in a few different colors:

  • Orange with black stripes
  • White with black stripes
  • Gold with faint tan stripes
