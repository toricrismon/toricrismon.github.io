Diet

Tigers eat many things including, but not limited to:

  • Large prey animals
  • Some small animals

After tigers have eaten what they can off of their prey, the will hide it and come back to it later


They often avoid larger animals that could easily overpower them, so they usually target lighter animals such as deer and wild pigs.
