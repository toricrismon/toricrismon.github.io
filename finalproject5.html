FUN FACTS

Now that I've taken you through this journey, I thought I should end this on a fun note and mention some facts about tigers that not many know.

  1. Tigers love water
  2. They can weigh up to almost 700 pounds!
  3. They live between 20-26 years old
  4. Their skin, like their fur, is striped!
